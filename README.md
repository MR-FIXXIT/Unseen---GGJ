# Unseen

An adventure puzzle game built in Unity, developed during **Global Game Jam 2026 – Kerala**.

## About
**Unseen** is an adventure puzzle game where players explore a forgotten cave in search of a hidden treasure. The journey begins when the protagonist discovers a mysterious mask and an ancient map leading deep into the cave. The mask grants the power of **Hidden Sight**, allowing players to reveal hidden symbols, concealed paths, and traces of the past that are invisible to the normal eye. Clues found without the mask are often incomplete, encouraging players to switch perspectives and uncover the true meaning behind the cave’s secrets.

## Core Mechanic – Hidden Sight
- Toggle the mystical mask to reveal hidden elements in the environment  
- Discover concealed symbols, alternate paths, and story clues  
- Solve puzzles by combining visible and hidden information  

## Gameplay Features
- Exploration-driven adventure gameplay  
- Perspective-based puzzle design  
- Environmental storytelling  
- Atmospheric cave setting  
- Designed and completed within a game jam time constraint  

## Game Jam
- **Event:** Global Game Jam 2026 – Kerala  
- **Diversifier:** The World is Alive  

## Platform
- MS Windows  

## Built With
- Unity  

## Language
- English  

## How to Play
- Explore the cave and observe your surroundings  
- Use the mask to activate **Hidden Sight**  
- Switch between normal vision and Hidden Sight to solve puzzles  

## Development Focus
This project focuses on rapid prototyping, clear gameplay mechanics, and immersive atmosphere. The Hidden Sight system forms the core gameplay loop and encourages curiosity, experimentation, and thoughtful exploration.
